About Me

Who Am I?

My name is Sina Hajimiri and I am an M.Sc. student in Artificial Intelligence at Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran. I am a researcher at the Machine Learning Lab in the Computer Engineering Department under the supervision of Prof. Soleymani.

My research is focused on Deep Representation Learning and Generative Models. I try to extend current generative models like VAEs and GANs and improve their sample generation and representation learning (for example by seeking disentanglement in the representation).
